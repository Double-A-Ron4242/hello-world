CREATION-SEC
  The Pillars of Triad Universe was an attempt into cobbling almost all of my settings in a single universe. And I do plan
on releasing the universe as a Creative Commons (which I'm trying to build a website to hold my content for the License of
Unmoddified Use for Commercial purposes). What started off as a concept essay to get into Brown College back in 2004 with
the game "The Axylon: Rights of Salvage" into an expansive and rich history of events in two galaxies (The Imperial galaxy
and the Kingdom galaxy). It is a Resident Evil on a starship style of over shoulder 3rd person view. And it is a new take of
zombie creation that makes this scary and a possibility of happening for real.

  This game is a bridge into the true story of the 1st Epic of Cycle One to Darain and HaVaK. Draciess is a world that Darkain
woke up on with amnesia, trying to figure out where is home. A world going through the era shift from the Medieval age into
the Renaissance era.Dragons are a part of society and even hold jobs. (But my dragons are mortal based and weaker than the
D&D true dragons.) But there has been told by the Great Seer Kihs-TOHR that a Messanger will arrive. This story is about
Darkain being that messenger, and the events that shaped the world of Draciess into being one of the strongest space society
within the Beta Quadrant of the Imperial galaxy.

  ***Please Take Note***
  The Pillars of Triad Universe is designed to be for everyone. "By the people, for the people." If you want to use the settings
and worlds in this Reality, please note of Canon Material (Cycles One, Two, and Three) and Sandbox Material (Cycle Four) will have
